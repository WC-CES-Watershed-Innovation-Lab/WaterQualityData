Water Quality Data

We collect water quality data continuously using instruments called AquaTROLLs. We collaborate with local community members to deploy AquaTROLLs on docks around the Chester River Watershed, where they measure water quality metrics like temperature, pH, dissolved oxygen, salinity, and turbidity. These instruments collect data every 20 minutes and send them to our computers wirelessly.

Using the drop-down menu below, select the water quality metric you want to view. Click the location legend to toggle which locations you want displayed. Click and drag the mouse to zoom in on a particular time window.
